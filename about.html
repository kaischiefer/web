About Kai

Kai Schiefer is an electrical and systems engineer focused on bridging embedded software, electrical architecture, and physical system design. His work spans EV harness design, embedded telemetry systems, and mechanical CAD exploration.

Outside of engineering consulting, Kai works on hands-on builds involving automotive systems, modeling, and experimental fabrication projects. He approaches both engineering and personal projects with a systems-first mindset: optimize structure, reduce complexity, and build for scalability.
