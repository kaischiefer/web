About Kai

Kai Schiefer is a mechatronics engineer with past history as an ADAS engineer. He has a bachelors in Mechanical Engineering, with a concentration in Software engineering and a Masters in Systems Engineering from the University of Michigan. His work spans Embedded systems, app development, wire harness design, mechatronics controls, 3d modeling, cnc, 3d printing, project management, product development, computer vision, and embedded telemetry systems.

Outside of engineering consulting, Kai works on hands-on builds involving automotive systems, modeling, and experimental fabrication projects. He approaches both engineering and personal projects with a systems-first mindset: optimize structure, reduce complexity, and build for scalability.
